Liudmila Te - About me

A few words about myself

My name is Liudmila Te. I was born in Russia and spent my early ages in Siberia. At the age of 5 my family moved to St-Petersburg where I live now.

I was an active child and tried a lot of activities, that's why during my school years I sang in school chorus, run in athlete team and acted in "Club of the Funny and Inventive People"(KVN).

In university I became interested in computer technologies and love to administrate my home Linux system. Later it evolved to web technologies and email development which I practicing nowadays.

And after all this years, I'm still eager to read and learn about some new technologies, test new features of well known HTML & CSS, create some pages or emails just for fun.

Even now, while creating this micro-site, I was excited to think about the structure, design (kind of), and I'm sure I will continue to update and change it. Just because I like the process and wanrt to see a good result.
